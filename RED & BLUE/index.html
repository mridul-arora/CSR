About Us

CSR is the integration of socially beneficial programs and practices into a corporation's business model and culture. UPES ACM believes in giving back to the world around. We establish activities can help forge a stronger bond between employee, corporation and the outer world, this boost their morale and feel more connected with the world around them. UPES ACM aims to increase long-term relationships by enabling them to become more efficient and attract positive attention for their efforts. Basically this means “Good company, Good people, Good environment.”

Our Mission

It is our mission to Increase the integration of sustainability into business models and management of efforts. Engage with more institutions about policy to drive the global agenda of poverty, education and self-improvement among each other and leveraging plans with the society.

Be a network which is recognised as a global leader. Provide learning aids and innovation opportunities to the global world to support their transformation and collaboration with us. It should be a clear way ahead because within the origin of the mission lie the ideals of the founder of the organisations.

Our Plan

The chapter conducted valuable CSR activities to reinforce its idea of sustainability. We believe in taking care of health and guaranteeing the best within us. Promoting the role and quality of giving back to the society.

Our state is filled with developmental sessions to encourage and motivate the society into believing that they too can DREAM BIG.

Integrity, Respect and Recognition, Passion for Excellence this is what lies within us.

Our Vision

A vision, a clear perception of what the company wants to achieve in the medium and long run.

We believe that CSR must also make itself explicit in a series of promises and public commitments which must not be broken or forgotten, because it means a framework of actions for correct and efficient decision making, that’s what is remembered!

Be a part of what we call a world of “humanitarian”.

The first CSR was conducted at the Teach Girls foundation, on the 11th of March 2018, after the fresh appointment of new CSR heads. Teach girls foundation is in an area at the slums on the bank of Rispana River. Make A Difference

The MAD CSR was conducted at Christian Retreat Center. The activity was successfully performed among 30 kids aged 12-13. It was an initiative to help those underprivileged children to bring them into the mainstream of knowledge and, at least, build up their interest in science, which they are still not aware of.

promoted an online fundraiser started by the Make-A-Difference foundation for the campaign for a children’s shelter at Rajpur Road. The CSR team visited the campaign for the same on the 1st of April 2018 including, some fun and technical activities to have an enjoyable, creative and interactive session with the kids. We had a project based activity there. Activity was mainly to make a quizboard using electricity. Children were first told about the materials required, explaining each material and its use specifically. After that they were guided, step-by-step, how to proceed and ultimately we came out with an excellent project.

But, as always, it has never been about the completing the project. It was and is all about making those children understand the importance of science and showing them the path of technology and building up their interests so they might find it easier to figure it out what they should go for because we understand that even the most vulnerable children in shelters are capable to create magic.
